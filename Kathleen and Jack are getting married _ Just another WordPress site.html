Our Story

Jack and Kat met on a fateful Thursday night outside a cheap & cheerful Vietnamese restaurant. She wore a dress and he wore brown shoes. Over hot bowls of Phō they talked as if they’d known each other for years and knew that they had found something incredibly special. From that night they became inseparable and fell madly in love.

Four months later they moved into together and Jack adopted Winnie as his own puppy. They decorated together, cooked together and created a wonderful life of their own. On Kat’s birthday in 2014, Jack proposed. Their wedding will be a joyful day filled laughter, dancing, good food and - most of all - love.
